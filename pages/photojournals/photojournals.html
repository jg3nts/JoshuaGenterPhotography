Sierra Dome Spider

The Sierra Dome Spider belongs to a family of sheet-web spiders known as Linyphiidae. Having a body that does not exceed 8mm, the Sierra Dome Spider is the largest in the Linyphiidae family.

An eye catching property of this type of spider is their interesting shaped web which is formed to act as a protective dome.
